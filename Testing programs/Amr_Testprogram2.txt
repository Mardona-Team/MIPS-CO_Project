Program #2: Implementing Xor
------------

C-code:
        int x,y,z;
        z=((!x)&y)|((!y)&x);

x-->$s0  y-->$s1

Assembly: 
          nor $t0,$s0,$zero   #!x
          nor $t1,$s1,$zero   #!y
          and $t2,$t0,$s1     #!x&y
          and $t3,$t1,$s0     #!y&x
          or  $s3,$t2,$t3
